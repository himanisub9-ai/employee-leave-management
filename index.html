Process
How LeaveIQ works

Four intelligent steps from leave request to approved coverage plan — all automated.

1
Receive Leave Request
Team member submits leave with dates, reason, and role. LeaveIQ captures all context automatically.
2
Check Calendar & Projects
Agent scans batch schedules, active client projects, and upcoming deadlines that overlap the leave window.
3
Score & Find Coverage
Generates a Low/Medium/High impact score and recommends the best available team member for coverage.
4
Auto-Draft Handoff & Approve
Handoff briefing document is auto-drafted and sent to both parties. Leave is approved instantly.

Features

Instant Impact Scoring
